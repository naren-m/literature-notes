# Spicy Garlicky Noodles (High Protein)

20-minute high-protein noodle bowl using high-protein ramen (Vite Ramen / Naked Noods). Lots of flavor, ground turkey, peppers, garlic.

## Method

1. Boil water for the noodles. Cook to package directions, then strain.
2. Crush garlic; slice bell pepper.
3. Whisk noodle sauce ingredients together in a bowl on a scale.
4. Wok over medium heat. Swirl peanut oil, then add garlic and red chili flake. Toast 30 seconds.
5. Add ground turkey, salt, MSG. Crank to high. Break up and cook until browned. Remove.
6. Spritz pan; toss in bell pepper with a pinch of salt. Stir-fry 60 seconds.
7. Return meat. Add noodle sauce — it will start to thicken.
8. When thickened, kill the heat. Add strained noodles; toss to coat.
9. Garnish with sesame oil, black sesame seeds, and cilantro.

## Ingredients

**Base**

- 212 g (2 bricks) Naked Noods (or high-protein ramen)
- 5 g peanut oil
- 3 cloves garlic, crushed
- Sprinkle Chinese red chili flake
- 227 g (½ lb) 93/7 ground turkey
- Salt, MSG to taste
- Pan spray
- 1 medium red bell pepper, thinly sliced (or any veg)

**Noodle sauce**

- 15 g soy sauce
- 30 g black vinegar
- 10 g sriracha
- 21 g hoisin
- 100 g water
- 5 g corn starch

**Garnish**

- 5 g sesame oil (optional)
- Cilantro
- Black sesame seeds

## Macros (per serving)

671 cal · 20 g fat · 3 g sat fat · 74 g carbs · 8 g fibre · 5 g sugar · 47 g protein.
