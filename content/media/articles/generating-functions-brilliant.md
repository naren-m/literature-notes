# Generating Functions — Brilliant Math & Science Wiki

Bookmark of the Brilliant wiki entry on generating functions, saved as a companion to the MIT OCW PDF in [[generating-functions]]. The original Notion page was a child of the Generating Functions note and contained only a back-mention to it.

## Link

- Brilliant wiki — [Generating Functions](https://brilliant.org/wiki/generating-functions/)

## Why it's useful

The Brilliant article is a friendlier on-ramp than the MIT OCW PDF: it walks through the definition, the intuition ("transforms problems about sequences into problems about functions"), and then gives worked examples with partial fractions, polynomial multiplication, and recurrence-relation solving — the same arc the main [[generating-functions]] note sketches, but with more exposition.
