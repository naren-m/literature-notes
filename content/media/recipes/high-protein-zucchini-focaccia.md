# High-Protein Zucchini Focaccia

1 tray = 2 portions. ~10 min prep, 60 min bake at 190 °C (fan).

## Ingredients

- 2 zucchini (600 g)
- 2 onions
- 2 garlic cloves
- 250 g flour
- 1 bunch cherry tomatoes (halved)
- 100 g almond protein
- 1 tsp Italian herbs
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tbsp oil for baking

## Method

1. Grate the zucchini, slice the onions, press the garlic.
2. Combine in a large bowl with the remaining ingredients.
3. Spread the dough on a baking sheet; drizzle with oil.
4. Bake 60 min at 190 °C (fan).
5. Cool completely before slicing.

## Macros (per portion)

777 kcal · 47 g protein · 106 g carbs · 16 g fat.
