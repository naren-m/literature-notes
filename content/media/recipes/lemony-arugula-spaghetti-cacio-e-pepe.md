# 🍲 Lemony Arugula Spaghetti Cacio e Pepe

## Ingredients

- Kosher salt
- 12 oz dried spaghetti
- ¼ lb pancetta or bacon, finely diced (~¾ cup)
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 tbsp minced garlic (~4 big cloves)
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes, plus more to taste
- 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper, plus more to taste
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
- 1½ cups freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano, plus more to serve
- 3 cups baby arugula

## Preparation

1. Cook spaghetti in heavily salted boiling water to al dente. Reserve **1 cup** pasta water; drain.
2. In a large skillet, cook pancetta over medium-high until crisped, 7–9 min. Add olive oil, then garlic, red pepper flakes, black pepper. Cook ~1 min until fragrant.
3. Add lemon juice. Toss in the pasta. Add Parm; toss, adding pasta water a couple of tablespoons at a time so the cheese coats the pasta. Add arugula; toss ~1 min until wilted.
4. Season with salt, more black pepper, and red pepper flakes. Serve with more Parm.
