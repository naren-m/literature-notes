# Oven-Baked Crispy Black Bean Tacos with Cilantro Yogurt Sauce

Crispy, flavorful, vegan tacos paired with a creamy cilantro yogurt dipping sauce — bean-hater to bean-lover material.

## Black bean tacos

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- ½ yellow onion, finely chopped (or 4 shallots)
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1–2 jalapeños, chopped and de-seeded
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 × 14 oz cans black beans, drained
- 1 tbsp taco seasoning
- Juice of 1 lime
- 6 large corn tortillas

## Cilantro yogurt sauce

- ½ cup soy yogurt or vegan Greek-style yogurt
- 1 bunch cilantro
- Juice of 1–2 limes
- 1 tsp olive oil
- ¼ tsp paprika
- Salt and black pepper to taste
