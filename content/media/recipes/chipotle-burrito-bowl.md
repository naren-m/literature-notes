# Chipotle Burrito Bowl (Copycat)

Build-your-own copycat of Chipotle's burrito bowl: rice, black beans, chicken, sweet corn salsa, tomato salsa, and guac. Recipe scales to 4 bowls.

## Ingredient notes

- **Adobo sauce** — drain from a can of chipotles in adobo, or use jarred adobo paste. To make adobo sauce: ~3 parts paste + 1 part water.
- **Oregano** — Italian or Mexican both work; flavors differ slightly.
- **Canned black beans** — biggest shortcut; you can also make Chipotle-style black beans or pinto beans from scratch.

## Components

### Chipotle-style chicken

1. Marinade: blend red onion, garlic, adobo sauce, ancho chili powder, olive oil, cumin, oregano in a food processor. Add a splash of water, then pour over boneless chicken thighs (or breasts) in a zip-top bag. Marinate ≥ 30 min, ideally overnight.
2. Cook in a skillet over medium-high heat with a little olive oil. Sear both sides until 165 °F internal. Rest, then chop into bite-sized pieces.

### Cilantro lime rice

1. Cook basmati in a 4:1 water:rice ratio. Bring water to a boil; add rice, salt, and a bay leaf. Cook 10–12 minutes until tender.
2. Drain, rinse with hot water in a fine-mesh sieve, then transfer to a bowl. Stir in lemon juice, lime juice, and chopped cilantro. Season with salt.

### Roasted chili-corn salsa

1. Cook frozen corn per package; spread on a sheet to cool.
2. Toss with chopped roasted poblano, red onion, cilantro, lemon and lime juice, and chopped jalapeño. Salt to taste.

### Fresh tomato salsa (pico de gallo)

1. Chop tomatoes, jalapeño, red onion, cilantro.
2. Combine with lemon juice, lime juice, and salt.

### Guacamole

1. Plan ~½ avocado per person. Mash flesh with lemon and lime juice.
2. Stir in chopped onion, cilantro, jalapeño, and a pinch of salt.

### Build the bowl

Rice → beans → chicken → salsas → corn → guac → cheese → lettuce → fork.

## Tips and variations

- **Yield:** scaled to 4 bowls.
- **Make ahead:** marinate chicken overnight; chop pico the day before; slow-cook beans in advance.
- **Leftovers:** rebuild bowls or make sheet-pan nachos.
- **Protein swaps:** barbacoa, steak, carnitas, sofritas (tofu).
- **Other salsas (in heat order):** fresh tomato (mild) → roasted chili-corn (medium) → tomatillo-green chili (medium) → red chili (hot).
- **Extras:** chipotle honey vinaigrette (turn it into a salad), fajita veggies, homemade tortilla chips.
