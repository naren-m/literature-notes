# Korean Vegetable Pancakes

Savoury Korean-style pancakes — crispy on the outside, packed with veg.

## Batter

- 1.5 cups flour
- 4 tbsp corn starch
- 1.5 cups water
- 1 tsp salt

## Vegetables (cut into thin strips/slices)

- 1 carrot
- 5 spring onions
- 1 onion
- 5 cabbage leaves
- ½ courgette
- Green pepper (optional)

## Method

1. Combine the batter ingredients.
2. Add the cut vegetables; stir.
3. Fry in a pan with oil until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
