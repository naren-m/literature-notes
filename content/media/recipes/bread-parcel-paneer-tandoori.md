# Bread Parcel — Paneer Tandoori Sandwich

## For the marination

- 3 tsp mustard oil
- 2 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
- 1 tsp garam masala powder
- 1 tsp black pepper powder
- ½ tsp cumin powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 4 tbsp curd (yogurt)
- 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
- Salt
- ¼ cup capsicum
- ½ cup onion
- ¼ cup tomato
- 1 cup paneer

## For the bread parcel

- Bread
- Garlic / Amul butter
- Cheese
- Tandoori mayo / mayonnaise

Marinate the paneer + veg in the spice-yogurt mix, fill bread with it, butter, cheese, mayo, and pan-toast (assemble like a stuffed bread parcel).
