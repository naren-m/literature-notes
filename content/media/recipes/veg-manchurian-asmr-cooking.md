# Veg Manchurian (ASMR Cooking)

Indo-Chinese vegetable Manchurian — crisp veg balls in a savory, spicy gravy.

## Manchurian balls

- Chopped cabbage — 3 cups
- Chopped carrot — 1 cup
- Spring onion greens — ½ cup
- Chopped garlic — 2 tsp
- Chopped ginger — 1 tsp
- Green chili — 2–3
- Salt — 1 tsp
- Black pepper powder — ½ tsp
- Corn flour — ½ cup
- All-purpose flour — ¼ cup
- Oil for frying

## Manchurian gravy

- Oil — 1 tsp
- Chopped garlic — ½ tsp
- Chopped ginger — ⅓ tsp
- Green chili — ½ tsp
- Onion — ⅓ cup
- Capsicum — ¼ cup
- Salt — ¼ tsp
- Black pepper powder — ¼ tsp
- Soya sauce — 1 tsp
- Vinegar — ⅓ tsp
- Red chili sauce — ½ tsp
- Tomato ketchup — 1 tsp
- Water — 1 cup

## Corn flour slurry

- Corn flour — 1 tsp
- Water — 3 tsp

## Method (general)

1. Mix the ball ingredients into a dough; shape into balls and deep-fry until golden.
2. In another pan, sauté the gravy aromatics, then sauces, water; thicken with the slurry.
3. Toss fried balls in the gravy; garnish with spring onion greens.
