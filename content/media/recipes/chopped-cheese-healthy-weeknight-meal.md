# Chopped Cheese (Healthy Version)

A NYC chopped-cheese sandwich, ready in 10 minutes — higher protein and lower fat than the classic. Not as good as the original, but very crushable.

## Method

1. Set a pan on medium-high heat with a spritz of baking spray.
2. Once hot, add ground meat and sprinkle with salt, a pinch of MSG, garlic powder, chile powder, smoked paprika, and a bit of onion powder. Chop everything together with a spatula.
3. While the meat cooks, slice cherry peppers, tomato, and lettuce.
4. Once the beef is cooked, pile it together in the shape of your bread. Top with the peppers and 2 slices of American cheese. Lid on, off heat — let the cheese melt.
5. Load up the bread with the meat and cheese. Top with tomatoes, salt, black pepper, then lettuce.
6. Wrap in parchment, slice in half, and devour.

## Approximate macros (per serving)

- ~593 cal
- 21 g fat
- 9 g sat fat
- 48 g protein
- 2 g fibre
- 1 g sugar
- 54 g carbs
