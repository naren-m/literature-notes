# Lasagne Slice — Zucchini, Eggplant & Mozzarella

Layers of zucchini, eggplant, spinach, and oozy mozzarella, served with a green salad. Cook 40 min, prep 20 min, serves 4. GF / NF / vegetarian.

## Ingredients

- 2 zucchini, sliced lengthways 0.5 cm thickness
- 1 small eggplant, sliced into 0.5 cm rounds
- 40 g baby spinach
- 2 cups mozzarella, shredded
- 2 tbsp parmesan, grated
- 2 tbsp basil pesto
- Cracked pepper to taste

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 200 °C fan. Grease and parchment-line a 23 × 14 cm baking tray.
2. Let eggplant sit 10 minutes; pat dry with paper towel.
3. Lay half the zucchini on the base, slightly overlapping.
4. Top with half the spinach, half the eggplant, half the mozzarella, dollops of pesto.
5. Continue with remaining zucchini, mozzarella, and parmesan.
6. Bake.

Serve with a green salad dressed in olive oil and apple cider vinegar.
