# Vegan Corn Zucchini Fritters

Serves 3.

## Ingredients

- 2 medium or 1 large courgette/zucchini
- 1 medium potato
- 1½ cups corn kernels (thawed or fresh)
- ½ onion, diced
- 1 cup chickpea flour
- ⅓ cup water
- 1 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper
- Olive oil for frying
- Vegan sour cream / aioli to serve
- Chopped chives and basil to serve

## Method

1. Grate zucchini and potato. Dice the onion.
2. Place grated zucchini and potato in a tea towel; gather the corners and squeeze out excess juice.
3. Combine drained vegetables with onion, chickpea flour, baking powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix.
4. Add the corn and water; mix until combined.
5. Pan-fry spoonfuls in olive oil until golden on both sides.
6. Serve with vegan sour cream / aioli, chives, basil.
