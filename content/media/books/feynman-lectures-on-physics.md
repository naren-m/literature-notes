# The Feynman Lectures on Physics

Bookmark for the free online edition of Feynman / Leighton / Sands's canonical undergraduate physics lectures, hosted by Caltech's Division of Physics, Mathematics and Astronomy. HTML5 edition with MathJax-rendered LaTeX and SVG figures.

## Links

- Online edition — [feynmanlectures.caltech.edu](https://www.feynmanlectures.caltech.edu/)
- Restore-last-view bookmark — [feynmanlectures.caltech.edu#restore](http://www.feynmanlectures.caltech.edu/#restore)

## Notes on use

- Free to read online, not to download in bulk. Rights to republish or distribute remain with Caltech.
- Requires JavaScript + MathJax (from mathjax.org); LocalStorage must be enabled.
- Oldest Internet Explorer supported is 9.0; modern Chrome/Firefox/Safari fine.
