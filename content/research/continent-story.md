# Continent Story — Animals & Landmarks by Continent

Quick reference of characteristic animals, rivers, and landmarks for each continent — originally a study aid for the user's daughter (Sahasra).

## Asia

- **Animals**: tigers, pandas, orangutans, horses.
- **Water bodies**: Yellow River, Dead Sea, Black Sea.

## Africa

- **Animals**: gorillas (tropical cloud forests).
- **Landmarks**: Pyramids of Giza, Mount Kilimanjaro.
- **Water bodies**: Red Sea.

## Europe

- **Landmarks**: Eiffel Tower, London Bridge.
- **Water bodies**: Volga River, Black Sea.
- **Animals**: snow bunting (songbird of the Arctic-circle tundra), red fox.

## North America

- **Landmarks**: Golden Gate Bridge.
- **Animals**: polar bear, caribou (reindeer).

## South America

- **Water bodies**: Amazon River.
- **Landmarks**: Machu Picchu.
- **Animals**: capybara — the world's largest rodent.

## Australia

- **Animals**: kangaroos, saltwater crocodiles, emus (the continent's largest bird).
- **Landscape**: Great Sandy Desert.

## Antarctica

- **Animals**: penguins, orcas (killer whales).
