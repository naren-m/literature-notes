# Black Pepper Tofu

Spicy, saucy black pepper tofu — converts even tofu skeptics. Serve over rice with sautéed broccoli or bok choy.

## Ingredients

- Extra-firm tofu (recipe also works with chicken or shrimp)
- Cornstarch
- Neutral cooking oil
- Lower-sodium soy sauce
- Granulated sugar
- Cracked black pepper
- Freshly grated ginger
- Garlic
- Shallots
- Fresh chile or red pepper flakes
- Scallions
