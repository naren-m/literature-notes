# Harmonic Series

Bookmark + attached PDF (`harmapa.pdf`) from UC Santa Cruz's Physics 116A archive. The original Notion page embedded the PDF; body content otherwise empty.

## What it covers

The harmonic series `H_n = 1 + 1/2 + 1/3 + ... + 1/n` and its asymptotic behaviour:

- `H_n ~ ln(n) + γ`, where `γ ≈ 0.5772` is the Euler–Mascheroni constant.
- Divergence of the infinite harmonic series despite the terms going to zero.
- Related alternating-series and `p`-series convergence tests.
