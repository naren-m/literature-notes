# Amarakosha — Chapter 1 (Prathama-kāṇḍa / Swarga-kāṇḍa)

The first *kāṇḍa* of the Amarakosha covers the **celestial and abstract** vocabulary of Sanskrit: gods, heavens, time, directions, speech, music, dance, mental faculties, snakes, hells, waters. It is organised into ten *vargas* (sections), each a verse-catalogue of synonyms for one semantic domain.

## Ten vargas of the Swarga-kāṇḍa

1. **Swargavarga** (स्वर्गवर्गः) — Heaven, deities, antigods, avatars of Viṣṇu, Brahmā, Śiva, Indra, Yama, Varuṇa, Kubera, Agni, Vāyu, and their epithets. Starts with the famous verse enumerating 18 synonyms for Svarga.
2. **Vyomavarga** (व्योमवर्गः) — Sky, atmosphere, clouds, rain, lightning, thunder, wind, mist. Covers meteorological vocabulary.
3. **Digvarga** (दिग्वर्गः) — Directions (cardinal and intermediate), the lords of the directions (dik-pālas), points on the horizon.
4. **Kalavarga** (कालवर्गः) — Time cycles: yugas, months, fortnights, days, hours, muhūrtas, tithis, nakṣatras, seasons (ṛtu), planets (graha), stars.
5. **Dhivarga** (धीवर्गः) — Mental faculties: intellect (buddhi), mind (manas), memory, knowledge, reasoning, emotions (rasa), feelings, colours (as subjective qualities).
6. **Shabdavarga** (शब्दवर्गः) — Speech and language: vāk, words, syllables, sounds, grammar terms, Vedic recitation, languages, poetry.
7. **Natyavarga** (नाट्यवर्गः) — Dance, music, drama: musical instruments, ragas, tala, dance gestures, dramatic genres.
8. **Patalabhogivarga** (पातालभोगिवर्गः) — Underworld and serpents (nāgas): pātāla-lokas, types of snakes, their kings (Vāsuki, Takṣaka, etc.), nāga mythology.
9. **Narakavarga** (नरकवर्गः) — Hells: types of narakas, the demons that rule them, the sufferings.
10. **Varivarga** (वारिवर्गः) — Waters: oceans, rivers (by name — Gaṅgā, Yamunā, Sarasvatī, etc.), lakes, ponds, springs, lotus types, aquatic creatures, fishes, dolphins, crocodiles.

## Sample opening (Swargavarga, verse 1)

The chapter opens with the famous "eighteen-synonyms-for-heaven" verse:

> **स्वर्-अव्ययं स्वर्गनाकत्रिदिवत्रिदशालयाः ।<br>
> सुरलोको द्योदिवौ द्वे स्त्रियां क्लीबे त्रिविष्टपम् ॥**

listing *svar / avyaya / svarga / nāka / tridiva / tridaśālaya / suraloka / dyu / div (fem.) / triviṣṭapa (neut.)* as 10+ synonyms for the celestial realm.
