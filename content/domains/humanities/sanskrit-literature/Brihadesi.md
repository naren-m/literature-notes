# Brihadesi

Wiki: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brihaddeshi

Early classical treatise on [[Carnatic music]] theory.

## Key Contributions

Defines [[Nada]] (sound) and its nature

Early systematic approach to rāga classification

Foundation for later works like [[Sangitaratnakaram]]

## Related Concepts

- [[Nada]] - Sound theory defined in Brihadesi
- [[Sangitaratnakaram]] - Later comprehensive treatise building on Brihadesi
- [[Katapayadi_Melakarta_Guide]] - Modern systematic rāga organization, including the chakra-based classification system
- [[Carnatic music]] - Classical music tradition
