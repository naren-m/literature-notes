# Meditations — Gregory Hays translation

Bookmark for Marcus Aurelius's personal journal (c. 170–180 CE), in the **Gregory Hays** translation — widely considered the most readable modern English edition.

Suggested from a Daily Stoic video; no notes on the Notion page beyond the Amazon bookmark.

## Why this edition

- Hays renders Marcus into *direct, modern English* without the Victorian formality of earlier translations.
- Excellent ~30-page introduction on Stoicism, Marcus's life, and why the *Meditations* exist at all (it was never written for publication — these are a Roman emperor's private notes to himself).

## Themes to re-read for

- Control vs. not-control (the dichotomy of control).
- *Memento mori* — the meditation on death as a clarifier.
- Logos, nature, and living according to reason.
- The view from above — perspective-shift as a tool.
