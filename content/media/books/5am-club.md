# The 5AM Club

Robin Sharma's *The 5AM Club: Own Your Morning. Elevate Your Life.* (2018). 3,073 Kindle readers highlighted the book per the Notion page — "Want to read" status; no personal notes yet.

## Thesis (popular framing)

Sharma argues that **the first hour of the day shapes the rest of the day**, and by extension your decade. The book is structured as a novelist-style parable (the billionaire, the entrepreneur, the artist) delivering the 20/20/20 formula.

## The 20/20/20 formula

Between 5:00 and 6:00 a.m.:

1. **5:00 – 5:20 — Move.** Intense exercise to flood the system with BDNF and reset cortisol.
2. **5:20 – 5:40 — Reflect.** Journalling, meditation, gratitude — anything introspective.
3. **5:40 – 6:00 — Grow.** Read, listen, study — deliberate skill / knowledge development.

## Surrounding concepts (Sharma's framing)

- *The 4 Interior Empires* — health, heart, mind, soul.
- *The Twin Cycles of Elite Performance* — high-excellence cycle (90 minutes deep work) + deep-refuelling cycle (recovery).
- *The Habit Installation Protocol* — 66 days to automaticity (matches James Clear's framing in [[atomic-habits]]).

## Caveats

The 5 a.m. specific time is cultural shorthand; the principle is **an uninterrupted early window for self-investment before the day's demands land**. Chronotype (see [[why-we-sleep]]) matters — late chronotypes pay a biological penalty for a 5 a.m. alarm.
