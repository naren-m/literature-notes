# Buffalo Chickpea Wraps

## Ingredients

- 1 (15 oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 2 cups cauliflower florets (~1 small head), 1-inch pieces
- 1½ tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp ground coriander
- ½ tsp dried thyme
- 1½ tsp garlic powder
- 2 tbsp avocado oil
- Salt to taste

## Sweet-and-spicy buffalo sauce

- 3 tbsp buffalo sauce
- 2 tbsp tahini
- 2 tbsp plain unsweetened dairy-free yogurt
- Juice of half a lemon
- 2 tsp maple syrup

## Assembly

- 3 burrito-sized tortillas
- Spinach
- Shredded carrots
- Optional dipping sauce

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 425 °F.
2. Pat chickpeas dry on a kitchen towel.
3. Place chickpeas and cauliflower on a large baking tray.
4. Whisk the spice blend; sprinkle with the oil and a generous pinch of salt over the chickpeas and cauliflower; toss.
5. Roast until crisp.
6. Whisk the buffalo sauce ingredients.
7. Build wraps: tortilla, spinach, carrots, roasted chickpeas/cauliflower, drizzle of sauce, roll.
