# Availability

Availability means systems, data, and services are reachable when legitimate users need them.

In the [[CIA Triad]], availability focuses on uptime and access. In [[STRIDE]], [[Denial of Service]] is the main threat against availability.

## Related Concepts

- [[Cryptography]] - trusted access depends on the surrounding security design staying available
- [[CIA Triad]] - the broader security model that includes availability
