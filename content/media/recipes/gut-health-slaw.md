# Gut Health Slaw

A mostly-raw, gut-friendly slaw with a 30-second vinaigrette.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups shredded purple cabbage
- 2 cups shredded green cabbage
- 1 cup thinly sliced or shredded fresh beets
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 1 cup thinly sliced fennel
- ½ cup chopped parsley

## Vinaigrette

- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- ¼ tsp each salt, pepper, garlic

## Method

1. Cut and prep all veggies (mandolin works well for fennel and cabbage; pre-shredded carrots are fine; thinly slice the beets).
2. Whisk dressing in a bowl, add to slaw, toss.
3. Optional: marinate overnight for stronger flavor.
4. Keeps 4 days in the fridge.
