# Habit formation stages

A four-stage model for forming habits, captured from reading but not yet rewritten in the user's own voice or tied to a single source. Treated here as literature, not as a permanent note.

## The four stages

1. **Noticing** — Use implementation intentions. Give each goal a specific time and place to live in the world.
2. **Wanting** — Don't rely on willpower. Use the environment to trigger the cue.
3. **Doing** — Repetition is the key at the start. Optimize for the beginning. Remove the friction to start.
4. **Enjoying** — Gamify habits. Give short-term rewards. Never miss two days in a row.
