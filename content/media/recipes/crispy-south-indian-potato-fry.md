# Crispy South-Indian Potato Fry

Curd rice with these crispy potatoes is the move. Simple and crispy potato fry to lift any lunch.

## Method

1. 250 g baby potatoes (or normal potatoes). Boil 1 whistle in cooker, or open-boil till cooked. Add ½ tsp salt while cooking.
2. Peel and chop the potatoes.
3. Pan: 1 tbsp oil + ½ tsp mustard seeds + ½ tsp urad dal + curry leaves. Let crackle.
4. Add 1 tsp chilli powder (mix Kashmiri and normal for color). Keep flame low so chilli doesn't burn.
5. Add boiled potatoes; sauté until everything is coated.
6. Keep on low, let it crisp up. Add salt to taste and ½ tsp cumin powder; mix.
7. Once raw smell goes and it turns crispy, kill the heat and serve.
