# Mediterranean Black Lentil Salad with Tahini Dressing

Chopped veggies and beluga lentils tossed with feta and a tahini dressing. Vegetarian, simple, fresh.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup black (beluga) lentils
- Persian / Lebanese cucumbers
- Red capsicum (bell pepper)
- Red onion
- Crumbled feta cheese
- Bunch of fresh parsley

## Tahini dressing

- Tahini
- White wine vinegar
- Dijon mustard
- Garlic clove
- Honey
- Salt + pepper
