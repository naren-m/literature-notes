# Chipotle Cashew Crema

A game-changing vegan crema. Full instructions and measurements at sweetsimplevegan.com.

## Ingredients

- Raw cashews, soaked
- Water
- Chipotle peppers in adobo
- Lime juice
- Smoked paprika
- Garlic powder
- Onion powder
- Salt

Blend until creamy.
