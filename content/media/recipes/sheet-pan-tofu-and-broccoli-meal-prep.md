# Sheet-Pan Tofu and Broccoli Meal Prep

A weekly meal-prep favorite — easy sheet-pan, plant-based.

## Ingredients

- 1 block extra firm tofu
- 3 cups broccoli florets
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and black pepper to taste

**Sauce**

- ⅓ cup coconut aminos
- 3 tbsp water
- 2 tbsp agave
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp arrowroot starch

## Method

1. Cube the tofu.
2. Press: wrap in a tea towel on a plate, weight with heavy plates. Press 20 min.
3. Preheat oven to 400 °F. Line a baking sheet with parchment.
4. Place pressed tofu and broccoli florets on the pan.
5. Drizzle olive oil; salt and pepper both.
6. Bake 22–24 min, tossing halfway, until tofu is browned.
7. While baking, make the sauce: combine all ingredients in a small saucepan and simmer until thickened.
8. Toss baked tofu and broccoli with the sauce.
