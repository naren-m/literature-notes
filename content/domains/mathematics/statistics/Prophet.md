# Prophet

Prophet is a forecasting method and library for [[Time series]] data with trend and seasonal structure.

It is often used when a business metric has calendar effects, holidays, trend shifts, or missing observations. Compared with lower-level statistical models, its practical appeal is that it exposes a workflow-oriented interface for fitting, generating future dates, and producing forecasts with uncertainty intervals.
