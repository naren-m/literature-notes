# 🍃 Spaghetti with Spinach Sauce

Quick, healthy, vibrant pasta sauce — wilted spinach blended with cheese into a creamy puree. Pairs with garlic bread and a simple salad for a weeknight dinner.

## Ingredients

- Spinach — roughly a panful
- 3 garlic cloves
- 3 peppers
- 1 onion
- 3 cups pasta (your choice)
- Grated cheese (for the sauce + extra to serve)
- Olive oil, salt, pepper

## Preparation

- Start a pot of water for the pasta and cook the spaghetti until just undercooked.
- Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high. Sauté the garlic.
- Add the spinach. Wilt with the garlic. Season with salt and pepper. Remove from heat and set aside.
- **Before** draining the pasta, reserve a cup of the pasta water.
- Drain the pasta; set aside.
- Transfer the spinach-garlic mixture to a food processor or blender.
- Add the cheese and half of the reserved pasta water.
- Purée until it forms a sauce, adding more pasta water if needed.
- Return the sauce to the pan; add the par-cooked spaghetti.
- Stir to combine and heat through, adding more pasta water if needed.
- Once the spaghetti is fully cooked, taste and re-season. Serve with extra grated cheese.

## Pasta cooking tips

- **Don't add oil to the cooking water** — the sauce will slip off the spaghetti.
- **Salt like you mean it.** Cooking water should taste like the ocean — this is the only opportunity to season pasta.
- **Wait for a rapid boil** before adding the spaghetti so it cooks properly.
- **Stir during cooking** to prevent clumping.
