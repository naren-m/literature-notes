# Vegan Vietnamese Summer Rolls

Vietnamese-restaurant-inspired summer rolls.

## Ingredients

- Rice paper wrappers
- Avocado
- Pepper (bell)
- Shredded carrots
- Optional protein: tofu
- Purple cabbage
- Lettuce
- Mint
- Peanut sauce (for dipping)

## Tip

Rinse rice wrapper in **hot** water for 5 seconds, then roll.
