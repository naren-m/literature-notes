# vyañjanā

one of the 3 [[sabda-vrittis]]

**3. व्यञ्जना — _vyañjanā_ — the suggestive / evocative function.** Beyond the denoted and the indicated, there remains a further layer that is _suggested_. Neither said directly nor substituted by convention — evoked. The delivered meaning is **vyaṅgyārtha** or simply **dhvani** ("resonance"). Ānandavardhana's thesis in the _Dhvanyāloka_'s opening verse is that **this** is the soul of poetry:

Similar to what [[tyagaraja swamy]] did in [[Bantu reethi Kolu]]
