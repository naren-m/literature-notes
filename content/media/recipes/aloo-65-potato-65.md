# Aloo 65 / Potato 65

Beginner-friendly Indian street snack — spice-coated deep-fried potatoes.

## Ingredients

- Potatoes — 500 g (chopped)
- Salt to taste
- Red chilli powder — 1 tbsp
- Garam masala — 1 tsp
- Coriander powder — 1 tsp
- Turmeric powder — 1 tsp
- Corn flour — 3 tbsp
- Rice flour — 1 tbsp
- Besan flour — 1 tbsp
- Red food colour — a pinch (optional)
- Water — 2 tbsp
- Oil for deep fry
- Green chillies — 5 (sliced)
- Curry leaves

## Method

Coat the chopped potatoes in the spice + flour mix moistened with water, then deep-fry. Toss with sliced green chillies and curry leaves to finish.
