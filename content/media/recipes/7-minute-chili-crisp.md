# 7-Minute Chili Crisp

Homemade chili crisp — adds flavor to almost everything. Makes 2 cups (halve as needed).

## Ingredients

- 1¼ cups grapeseed, avocado, or vegetable oil
- ½ cup thinly sliced shallots
- 8 large cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1 cup crushed red pepper (red chili flakes)
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp tamari or soy sauce
- 2 tsp grated or finely minced ginger
- ½ tsp salt (or to taste)

## Method

1. Place shallots, garlic, and oil in a microwave-safe dish. Microwave uncovered ~7 minutes (or one minute at a time) until shallots and garlic are golden and crispy. Cut shallots/garlic similar thickness so they cook evenly.
   - Alternative: simmer stovetop in a saucepan.
2. Cool 1 minute.
3. Add crushed red pepper, cinnamon stick, maple syrup, tamari, ginger, and salt. Mix and let infuse.
