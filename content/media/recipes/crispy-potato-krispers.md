# Crispy Potato Krispers

Easy fried potato sticks — addictive snack.

## Ingredients

- 5 boiled potatoes
- Cornflour — ⅓ cup (or a little more for dough consistency)
- Spring onions
- Carrot (chopped, optional)
- Salt and pepper
- Oil to fry

## Method

Mash boiled potatoes with cornflour, salt, pepper, spring onions, and chopped carrot. Shape into sticks and deep-fry until crispy.
