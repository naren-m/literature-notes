# Buddhi

Buddhi is the faculty of determination and discernment. Where [[Manas]] considers alternatives, buddhi decides what is true or what should be done.

In [[NirvanaShatakam]], buddhi is named among the inner instruments that are negated as the Self. The verse does not reject buddhi as useless; it rejects mistaking the deciding faculty for the ultimate "I".

## Related Concepts

- [[Manas]] - deliberation and alternatives
- [[Ahamkara]] - the "I-maker" that claims ownership
- [[chitta]] - memory and stored impressions
- [[NirvanaShatakam]] - negates buddhi as the final Self
- [[HanumaStuthi]] and [[Hanuman Chalisa]] - use buddhi as a desired faculty of clarity
