# Mexican Black Bean Bowl

## For the beans

- 1 tin or jar of black beans (Bold Bean Co)
- 1 white or pink onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 green chilli
- 1 tsp each: salt, smoked paprika, cumin, ground coriander
- Handful of fresh coriander (cilantro)

## Tahini dressing

- 1 cup tahini
- 1 tsp chipotle paste
- 1 pinch smoked salt
- 1 lime

## To serve

- Avocado
- Pickled red onion
- Jalapeños
- Lime
- Paprika
- Fresh coriander
- Rice

## Method

1. In a pan, sauté the veggies until translucent; add the aromatics.
2. Add the black beans with their liquid; simmer 20 minutes.
3. Stir in fresh coriander before serving.
4. Whisk dressing ingredients; thin with water as needed.
5. Serve over rice with toppings of choice.
