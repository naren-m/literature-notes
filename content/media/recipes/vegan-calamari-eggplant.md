# Vegan Calamari (Eggplant)

Eggplant disguised as calamari. 3–4 servings, ~25 min.

## Ingredients

- 2 eggplants — peel, slice, then punch out the centers with a bottle cap to get rings
- Water
- 2 tbsp soy sauce or vegan fish sauce
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 sheet nori
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Method

1. Cook the eggplant rings in the seasoned brine 5 minutes. Drain.
2. Make batter: ½ cup (60 g) flour + salt and pepper to taste + ½ tsp garlic powder + ¾ cup (180 ml) plant milk. Whisk.
3. Coat eggplant with batter, then with breadcrumbs.
4. Fry in oil until crispy.
