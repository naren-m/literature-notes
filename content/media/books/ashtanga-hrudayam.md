# Ashtanga Hrudayam

Classical Ayurveda compendium by Vāgbhaṭa (ca. 7th century CE). The Notion page is a stub: a reading bookmark pointing at two PDF links and four Telugu-edition bookmarks collected during the "want to read" phase.

## What it is

*Aṣṭāṅga Hṛdayam* ("Heart of the Eight Limbs") is one of the three foundational texts of classical Ayurveda (the *Bṛhat-trayī* — the Great Triad — alongside *Caraka Saṃhitā* and *Suśruta Saṃhitā*). Organised around the eight branches of Ayurvedic practice.
