# 🍲 Broccoli Bolognese with Orecchiette

## Ingredients

- 1 large head of broccoli, cut into florets
- Kosher salt
- 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
- 4 garlic cloves, smashed
- 12 oz fresh Italian sausage (~3 links), casings removed
- Crushed red pepper flakes
- 12 oz orecchiette
- 3 tbsp unsalted butter, cut into pieces
- 1½ oz Parmesan, finely grated (~1½ cups), plus more for serving

## Preparation

1. Cook broccoli in salted boiling water until crisp-tender, ~3 min. Slotted-spoon to a colander; cool. **Save the pot of water** for the pasta. Chop broccoli small; set aside.
2. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a large skillet over medium. Cook smashed garlic, shaking the pan occasionally, until starting to turn golden, ~2 min. Add sausage and a generous pinch of red pepper flakes; break up with a wooden spoon. Cook 6–8 min until browned and cooked through.
3. Return the pasta water to a boil and cook orecchiette to barely al dente, ~9 min (set timer 3 min less than package — pasta will finish in the skillet).
4. Ladle ~½ cup pasta water into the skillet with the sausage; add blanched broccoli. Keep at a low simmer, stirring and mashing.
5. Spider or slot the pasta into the skillet, then ladle in another ½ cup pasta water. Cook ~4 min until pasta absorbs most of the liquid and is just al dente. Stir in butter until melted; transfer to a large bowl. Gradually add the Parm, tossing constantly to make a glossy emulsified sauce.
6. Serve topped with more Parm, red pepper flakes, and a drizzle of oil.
