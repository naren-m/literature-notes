# Korean Cucumber Salad (2-Minute)

Low-calorie, super-spicy and flavorful Korean cucumber salad. Part of a "summer salad series".

## Ingredients

- 3 cucumbers, roughly chopped
- ½ tbsp dark soya sauce
- 1 tbsp chilli flakes
- 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
- Some fresh spring onion greens
- ½ tbsp olive oil
- ½ tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp salt

## Method

1. Combine all ingredients.
2. Refrigerate 15 minutes before serving.
