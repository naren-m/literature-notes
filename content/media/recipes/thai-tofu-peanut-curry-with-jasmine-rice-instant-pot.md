# Thai Tofu Peanut Curry with Jasmine Rice (Instant Pot)

One-pot vegan and gluten-free Thai peanut curry — rice cooks in the Instant Pot at the same time as the curry (pot-in-pot).

## Pre-prep

- Drain water from the tofu package and rinse the block.
- Place a heavy pan (cast iron) on top of the tofu and press for 15 minutes to remove excess liquid. Cube.

## Curry method (Instant Pot)

- Sauté chopped onions, bell pepper, and spinach for a minute. Add ¼ cup water and deglaze the pot.
- Add salt, the coconut-peanut sauce mixture, tofu cubes, and ¾ cup water. Stir.
- Place the trivet + rice container with rinsed jasmine rice on top.
- Lock the lid; pressure-cook on Manual/High for **5 minutes**.
- After the beep, natural release for 10 minutes, then quick-release.
- Lift out the rice and trivet.
- Serve hot, garnished with chopped roasted peanuts, green onions, and cilantro.

## Stovetop variation

- Heat oil; sauté onion, ginger, garlic for a minute.
- Add salt, coconut-peanut sauce mixture, tofu cubes, ¾ cup water. Mix.
- Cover and cook 10 minutes on medium flame. Stir.
- Taste and adjust seasoning.

## Storage

- **Fridge:** 5 days. Add a splash of water when reheating if too thick.
- **Freezer:** freezes well. Cool completely; freeze in single-portion containers. Tofu may go slightly spongy.

## Variations

- Add cooked chickpeas for more protein.
- Swap veggies — anything in the fridge works.
- Yellow or green curry paste in place of red.
- Almond / other nut butter instead of peanut.
- For more heat: Thai chilies, jalapeño, Serrano, more paste, red pepper flakes, sambal, sriracha.
- Paneer in place of tofu (non-vegan).
- Tahini in place of peanut butter (nut allergy).
- Kale, chard, or arugula in place of spinach.

## Nutrition (per serving, full meal incl. rice)

~470 kcal · 58 g carb · 18 g protein · 19 g fat · 7 g sat fat · 1102 mg sodium · 5 g fibre · 12 g sugar.
