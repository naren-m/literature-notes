# 1-Ingredient Protein "Popcorn" — Roasted Cannellini Beans

Roasted, seasoned cannellini / kidney beans that "pop" in the oven — protein- and fibre-loaded snack or salad topper.

## Ingredients

- 2 cans cannellini (white kidney) beans, rinsed and drained
- 2–3 tbsp EVOO
- 1 tsp salt + pepper to taste

**Toppings**

- Rosemary (finely chopped)
- Tajín spice

## Method

Bake at 425 °F for 35–45 minutes.

## Dipping sauce

- ¼ cup tahini
- 3 tbsp ACV
- ¼ tsp Tajín spice
