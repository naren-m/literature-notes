# Hummus — Gaza-Style

Inspired by hummus from a Gaza City café (2019). Serves 6 as a meze.

## Ingredients

- 200 g chickpeas (uncooked)
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 200 g tahini
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 cloves garlic
- A small handful of parsley leaves
- 1 tbsp paprika
- Extra virgin olive oil to serve
- Breads and veggies for dunking
- Salt

## Method

1. Soak chickpeas overnight in plenty of water. Drain and rinse.
2. Return to the pan; cover with plenty of water and add baking soda. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
3. Reduce to a simmer, skimming scum, and cook 1–1½ hours until tender.
4. Drain, **reserving the chickpea cooking water** in a jug.
5. Reserve a few whole chickpeas for garnish; blend the rest with tahini, lemon juice, garlic, parsley, salt, and chickpea water until smooth.
6. Plate, dust with paprika, dollop with reserved chickpeas, drizzle olive oil, garnish with parsley.
7. Serve with bread and veggies.
