# Black Bean Quesadilla

Veganuary-friendly quick lunch quesadillas. 3 servings, ~15 min.

## Filling

- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 carrot
  > sauté in oil 3–4 min
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp each cumin and paprika
- Salt to taste
- Optional: ½ tsp chili powder
- 1 can (220 g) black beans
  > stir-fry 2–3 min, then mash to a chunky paste

## Sauce

- 3 tbsp vegan yogurt
- 3 tsp vegan mayo
- 3 tbsp chopped roasted peppers (jarred)
- ½ lime, juiced
- ½ tsp chili powder
- Salt to taste
  > blend smooth

## Assembly

- 3 small tortillas
- Spread some bean filling and the sauce on one half of a tortilla.
- Fold and fry each side until golden brown.
