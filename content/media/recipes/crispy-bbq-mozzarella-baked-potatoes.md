# Crispy BBQ Mozzarella Baked Potatoes

## Ingredients

- 6 baby potatoes
- Paprika, salt, chili, pepper
- Olive oil
- Chopped parsley
- Mozzarella cheese
- BBQ sauce (to finish)

## Method

1. Boil potatoes in a pot until soft. Cool.
2. Marinate with paprika, chili, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Mix well.
3. Flatten with a cup; top with mozzarella.
4. Bake at 180 °C until golden and the cheese is melted.
5. Top with chopped parsley and BBQ sauce.
