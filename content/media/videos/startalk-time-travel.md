# StarTalk @ NY Comic Con: It's About Time!

StarTalk live panel on the physics of time travel. Core takeaways:

## Notes

- Time travel is *theoretically* possible — the equations of general relativity don't forbid it.
- **Wormholes** can connect one point in space to another. Passing through a wormhole would move you forward or backward in *time*, depending on the direction of traversal.
- Going back in time changes things, spawning a new timeline — essentially the *Flash*-verse picture of branching universes.
- **Stephen Hawking**'s [[chronology-protection-conjecture]] proposes that the laws of physics conspire to *prevent* spawning new universes by altering the past — sometimes informally framed as a "Chronology Projection Agency."
