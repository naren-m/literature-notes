# Air-Fried Cauliflower Bites

Crispy air-fried cauliflower for a dinner side.

## Ingredients

- 1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets
- 3 tbsp olive oil (Aficio)
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- ¾ tsp paprika
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp pepper

## Method

1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl; mix to coat well.
2. Air-fry at 400 °F for 12 min, shaking halfway.
