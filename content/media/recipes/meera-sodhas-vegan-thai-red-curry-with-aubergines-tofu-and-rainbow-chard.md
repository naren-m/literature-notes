# Thai Red Curry with Aubergines, Rainbow Chard, and Tofu

A Meera Sodha vegan red curry built around dried red chillies (Kashmiri or Thai), softened with coconut milk. Ear-ticklingly spicy and complex.

**Prep:** 15 min · **Cook:** 45 min · **Serves:** 4

## Ingredients

- 3 aubergines (~900 g), cut into 6cm × 2cm wedges
- Rapeseed oil
- 8 large dried chillies (Kashmiri or Thai)
- 1 banana shallot, peeled and chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, peeled and chopped
- 1 tbsp galangal paste (Bart's or Thai Taste)
- 1 tsp white peppercorns (or ½ tsp ground white pepper)
- 2 stalks fresh lemongrass, bases only, chopped
- 8 makrut lime leaves, centre stem and stalk removed, finely shredded
- 1½ tsp fine sea salt
- 2 × 400 ml tins coconut milk
- 1 × 280 g pack tofu (Tofoo)
- 1 tbsp coconut or dark brown sugar
- 1½ tbsp light soy sauce
- 200 g rainbow chard, stems chopped and leaves shredded

**To serve:** Thai basil, picked leaves; steamed jasmine rice.

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 220 °C (200 °C fan)/425 °F/gas 7 and line two large oven trays. Toss aubergine wedges with 2–3 tbsp oil; spread out on the trays. Roast 20 minutes, turning once, until starting to burnish.
2. Make the curry paste. Cut tops off the chillies and shake out the seeds. Soak the deseeded chillies in just-enough boiling water for 5 minutes.
3. In a blender, combine shallot, garlic, galangal paste, pepper, lemongrass, 4 lime leaves, and salt. Add the soaked chillies and 5 tbsp of their soaking water; blitz to a smooth paste.
4. Drain and press tofu, pat dry, and cut into 2 cm cubes.
5. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a large pan over medium heat. When very hot, fry the tofu cubes 5 minutes, turning often, until golden. Transfer to a plate; leave the oil behind.
6. Lower heat slightly. Add the curry paste and remaining lime leaves; fry 5 minutes. Slowly pour in the coconut milk, stirring the paste in as you go.
7. Add soy sauce and sugar; bring to a boil. Lower heat; add roasted aubergine, fried tofu, and chard. Simmer 5 minutes until the chard wilts.
8. Off heat. Serve with jasmine rice and Thai basil leaves.
