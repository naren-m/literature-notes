# Dad's Famous Salsa

Insanely easy salsa — pop everything in the blender and call it a day.

## Ingredients

- 2 (10 oz) cans diced tomatoes with green chilies
- ½ white onion, diced
- 1 jalapeño, sliced
- ½ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- Juice of 2 limes
- 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp freshly cracked black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Tortilla chips, for serving

## Method

Add all ingredients to a blender and pulse about 10 times, maintaining a chunky texture. Serve with chips.
