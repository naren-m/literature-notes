# Spinach Artichoke Dip — Instant Pot and Oven

Creamy, rich, vegetarian appetizer dip — frozen spinach, marinated artichokes, Italian cheese blend cooked together. Low-carb / keto / gluten-free; can be made vegan with cashew cream + nutritional yeast or plant-based cream cheese.

## Ingredient idea

A mix of cream cheese, sour cream, frozen spinach, artichoke hearts, Italian cheese blend, minced garlic, and all-purpose seasoning. Optional: a tablespoon of chopped Giardiniera for a pickled-jalapeño-and-carrot accent.

For low-calorie: use low-fat cream cheese, low-fat sour cream, and reduced-fat cheese blend.

For vegan: swap cream cheese for cashew cream / Kite Hill almond cream cheese; use nutritional yeast and vegan cheese.

## Instant Pot method

1. Pour broth into the inner pot. Add minced garlic, cream cheese, frozen spinach, sour cream, and seasoning.
2. Drain artichokes and add to the pot. Close the lid.
3. Set to **Manual / Pressure Cook on High for 4 minutes**, vent at SEALING.
4. After the beep, wait a few minutes, then quick-release.
5. Stir in the Italian cheese blend.
6. Transfer to a serving bowl. Garnish with parsley, red chili flakes, or black pepper.
7. The dip thickens as it cools. If too runny, finish on Sauté for a few minutes.

(Oven instructions are also available on the source page.)

## Serving suggestions

- Toasted bread: baguette slices, breadsticks, pita chips, crostini, naan, tortilla chips.
- Fresh veggies: celery, broccoli, carrots, radishes.
- Fancy version: serve in a bread bowl, or scoop over cooked macaroni for fancy mac & cheese.
- Salt-crystal soft pretzel bites are a great match.
