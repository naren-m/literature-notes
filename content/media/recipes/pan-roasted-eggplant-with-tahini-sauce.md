# Pan-Roasted Eggplant with Tahini Sauce

Quick stovetop alternative to oven-roasted eggplant. ~20 min, 3–4 servings.

## Eggplant

- 2 eggplants — cut into wedges, salt heavily, rest 10 min, pat dry
- Heat oil in a pan; fry on medium-high until charred and soft
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp agave
- Salt to taste — toss

## Tahini sauce

- 4 tbsp tahini
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp sriracha
- Salt to taste
- Water to thin

## Serve

Spoon sauce over the eggplant.
