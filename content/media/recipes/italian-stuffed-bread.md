# Italian Stuffed Bread

Easy-to-assemble stuffed bread for lunch, brunch, or appetizer. Remove the bread guts to fit more inside.

## Ingredients

- 1 × 13-inch loaf of thick crusty bread
- 8 slices fresh mozzarella
- 2 ripe Roma tomatoes, sliced thin
- 1 tsp fresh or dry oregano
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, grated
- ¼ tsp salt
- 3 or 4 slices prosciutto, ham, or turkey
- ½ cup pesto

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 350 °F.
2. Slice the loaf in half lengthwise.
3. Remove most (but not all) of the bread from the bottom — careful not to break the crust.
4. Layer: 4 slices mozzarella → tomato → olive oil → oregano → 2 cloves grated garlic → salt → prosciutto → remaining 4 slices mozzarella → pesto.
5. Brush the top of the loaf with olive oil; grate 1 clove garlic over.
6. Bake the bottom for 20 minutes (until cheese is melted and bread is golden).
7. Bake the top for ~10 minutes until golden.
8. Reassemble, slice, serve.
