# Moroccan-Style Creamy Veg Soup

Tray-roasted veg, ras el hanout and ground ginger, served with crispy halloumi croutons. Serves 6.

## Ingredients

- 500 g squash, cut into 2 cm cubes
- 200 g cauliflower florets and roughly chopped stalk
- 4 tomatoes, halved
- 2 red peppers, roughly chopped
- 2 red onions, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 aubergine, cut into 2-inch chunks
- 1 bulb of garlic, head bit cut off
- 5 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
- 2 tbsp tomato purée
- 2 tsp ras el hanout
- 2 tsp ground ginger
- 400 g tin coconut milk
- 750 ml vegetable stock
- Juice of ½ lemon
- A large handful of coriander leaves
- 250 g block halloumi
- 15 g toasted pine nuts
- Salt and pepper

## Method

1. Pre-heat oven to 200 °C (fan). Tray up the squash, cauliflower, tomatoes, peppers, onions, aubergine, and garlic. Drizzle with 4 tbsp olive oil, season, roast.
2. After roasting, blend with stock, coconut milk, tomato purée, ras el hanout, ginger.
3. Adjust seasoning with lemon juice, salt, pepper.
4. Pan-fry halloumi until crisp.
5. Serve soup topped with halloumi, coriander, and toasted pine nuts.
